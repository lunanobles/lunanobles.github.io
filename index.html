Lunar Creative Course-Catalog

A collection for all courses offered by Lunar Creative.

Lunar Creative is a educational program in the game RecRoom meant to teach those who wish to create UGC (User-Generated Content) for the platform. The Program features two main categories, Pathed and Loose, both with their own subcategories ranging from topics like Building, to Content Creation, to Rooms. Pathed Type courses follow a strict path of 101 and beyond, there are 4 strands of courses Creation, Curcuits, Rooms, and RecRoom. An example of one of the strands can be seen in the table below:

Strand101102103104111112121122
RoomsBasic SettupRolesSettingsSubroomsTemplatesRooms 1.0 & 2.0ClubhousesDorm Rooms

The Loose Type, however, has many different possable option and routes that you could take. Many courses require you to have take its predecessor before you take it (listed under 'prerequisites'). There are still many courses that do not have any prerequisites, including all courses in the 'Other Loose Courses' subcategory, and any 'Micro' or 'Foundations' course. This webpage includes navagation links to Pathed course, its listing (larger screens only), Loose Courses, its four subcategories (larger screens only), and Contact information. There is also a range slider to change the fontsize of the page (larger screens only), used for accessibility.

How to Enroll & get Certificates:

For interested students to enroll in LunarCreative, they must visit the Discord Server and enter the 'registar' channel, where they have the ability to chose either Pathed or Looses Courses. After choosing Pathed, students pick a strand, and should then notify a admissions staff after each completion of a course; once students have completed all courses in a strand, they will be given a final project assignment that varies from strand to strand, after completion the student will earn their strand certificate! After choosing Loose, students will select from any of the courses, a admissions staff will verify they qualify for the course, then after completion of the course, students should notify admissions staff; after completing any course with a certificate attached (if the student decides to go for the certificate) they will be given a final project assignment, after completion the student will earn their certificate!

Rules & Expectations

More information about rules can be found in the Discord Server. Both students and staff should adhere to the rules and expectations of LunarCreative, breakage of said rules is punishable by expulsion and/or certificate stripping.

Loose Courses:

Makerpen & Animation:

Makerpen & Animation, also known a MA

The Makerpen & Animation category of the Loose type courses is a program of courses offered by LunarCreative to teach students various skills in fields like Creative Design, Storytelling, and Computer Science. This category focuses on the Makerpen, its tools, and Creative Design. It achieves this by first teaching the foundational skills of MA in the course MA Foundations, where students learn how to navigate, create, and think like an RR Creator. After learning the foundational skills, students may choose to focus in either Animation or Large Scale Building in the sister courses MA for Animation and MA for Large Scale Building. Finally, Students can finalise their education in MA with the MA Expertise course, where they learn how to work in more fields, develop more skills down-the-line, and create a Final Project demonstrating their new-found skills in Makerpen & Animation! This Category also offers a CrashCourse called Micro-MA. There is also the course Post-MA that teaches students how to be ready for working as a RR creator.
